Published Research

IJSRM Publication

Applications and Impacts of Internet of Things (IoT) in Logistics Industry in Vietnam

Research Overview

Conducted an in-depth study on how IoT technologies are transforming Vietnam's logistics sector, focusing on applications such as real-time tracking, smart warehousing, predictive maintenance, and supply chain optimization.

Key Findings

The research highlighted both opportunities and challenges, including cost efficiency, transparency, and operational resilience, while also addressing barriers like infrastructure readiness and data security. Findings emphasized IoT's potential to enhance the competitiveness of Vietnamese logistics firms in the context of Industry 4.0 and global trade integration.
